Questions

Financing FAQ

Will checking my rate hurt my credit?

No. Affirm, Klarna and our lease-to-own partners use a soft credit check to prequalify, which never affects your score. A hard pull only happens if you accept an offer and move forward.

Is there really 0% APR?

Yes, on qualifying builds and approved credit — most commonly through Affirm and Klarna's pay-in-4. The 0% offers you're eligible for show up during prequalification.

What if I have bad credit or no credit?

Snap Finance and Acima are lease-to-own options that look beyond your FICO score — many customers with thin or damaged credit still get approved, often up to $5,000 or more.

How much can I finance?

Most lenders approve typical wheel & tire packages instantly. For larger duallie builds over $8,000 or fleet orders, talk to a specialist about our extended in-house terms.

Can I pay it off early?

Absolutely — and you should. Affirm charges simple interest with no prepayment penalty, and the lease-to-own plans offer early-purchase discounts that save you money.
